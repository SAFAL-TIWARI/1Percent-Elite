Frequently Asked Questions

What is 1% Elite Vault?
1% Elite Vault is a centralized platform for academic resources, notes, previous year question papers, and essential learning materials, curated to assist students in their educational journey.
How do I report a missing resource or broken link?
You can easily let us know by using the contact form above. Just select "Website Issue" or "Resource Request" as the subject and provide the details.
Is 1% Elite Vault free to use?
Yes! Our platform is entirely free. We believe in open access to educational resources to help all students succeed in their academics.
I have an issue with the mobile app, what should I do?
If you encounter any bugs or issues with the 1% Elite mobile app, please select "App Issue" from the dropdown above and provide details such as your phone model, Android/iOS version, and a description of the problem.
